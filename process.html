The Process

Four steps from
enquiry to funded.

fundr handles the hard work — you just tell us what you need and choose the option that suits your business. Most clients are approved within 24 hours of submitting their details.

1

Step One

Tell us what you need

Share what your business is looking to finance and what you're trying to achieve. Whether it's a single vehicle, a piece of equipment, or a larger fleet — give us the details and we'll take it from there. No lengthy forms, no jargon.

Vehicle or asset type Approximate value Business details Your goals
2

Step Two

We review and go to market

Nick personally reviews your application and takes it to the right lenders on our panel. We know which lenders suit which assets, industries and business profiles — so we don't just submit and hope. We structure your deal to give it the strongest possible chance of approval.

3

Step Three

We present your options

Once we have lender responses, Nick walks you through the options in plain English — rates, terms, repayments, and which option makes the most sense for your situation. No pressure, no upselling. Just clear, honest advice so you can make the right decision for your business.

Clear comparison Plain English explanations Honest recommendation

Step Four

Sign, settle, and get moving

Choose the option that works for you, sign the documentation, and we handle everything through to settlement. Once settled, the asset is yours — and fundr remains your point of contact for any questions, refinancing, or future finance needs.

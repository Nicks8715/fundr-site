Services We Provide

fundr is a specialist asset finance brokerage. We provide financial adviser services in relation to asset finance products, including:

  • Vehicle finance (cars, utes, vans, trucks)
  • Equipment finance
  • Business finance and working capital

We do not provide financial advice in relation to investment products, insurance, mortgages, or KiwiSaver.

Our Lender Panel

fundr works with a panel of 15+ New Zealand lenders, including banks and specialist non-bank asset finance companies. We submit applications to lenders on your behalf and identify the lender most suited to your situation and requirements.

We do not have any ownership interest in any lender on our panel, and no lender has any ownership interest in fundr.

How We Are Paid

fundr is primarily compensated by lenders through commission payments when a finance application is settled. The commission rate varies by lender and product type. In some cases, a broker fee may also be charged to clients — this will always be disclosed and agreed upfront before any application is submitted.

Our commission arrangements do not prevent us from acting in your best interests. We recommend lenders based on which lender is the best fit for your specific deal and circumstances, not which lender pays the highest commission.

Conflicts of Interest

As a broker compensated by lenders, fundr has a potential conflict of interest in that we may receive different commission rates from different lenders. We manage this conflict by:

  • Always recommending the lender and structure that best fits your needs
  • Being transparent about our remuneration arrangements on request
  • Having a formal conflicts of interest policy

Duties

fundr and its representatives are bound by duties under the Financial Markets Conduct Act 2013, including the duty to:

  • Meet the standards of competence, knowledge, and skill
  • Give priority to your interests
  • Exercise care, diligence, and skill
  • Comply with the Code of Professional Conduct for Financial Advice Services

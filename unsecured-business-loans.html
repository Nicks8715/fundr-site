Before You Apply

What strengthens
your application.

Unsecured lenders assess you on turnover, banking conduct and cash flow rather than an asset. Here's what generally helps — and what can make approval harder.

✓Consistent, steady monthly turnover
✕Sporadic or declining revenue
✓A clear purpose for the funds
✕Unclear or high-risk use of funds
✓Clean banking conduct
✕Dishonours or bounced payments
✓Cash flow comfortably covers repayments
✕Tight or negative cash flow
✓Acceptable credit history
✕Recent defaults or high-risk credit behaviour
✓No existing short-term debt stacking
✕Multiple short-term facilities already in place

Every lender weighs these differently, and none of this is a hard rule. Talk to Nick about where your business sits — we'll tell you honestly which lender gives you the best shot.

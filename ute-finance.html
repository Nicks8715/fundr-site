  • Most applications receive a decision within 24 hours. In many cases we can get same-day approval. Once approved, funds are typically released within 1–2 business days so you can pick up your ute quickly.
  • Yes. fundr can finance both new and used utes, including private sales. Used ute finance is subject to the age and condition of the vehicle — most lenders will consider utes up to 10–12 years old depending on the loan amount.
  • For business use, ute finance repayments — and in some structures, depreciation — can be tax deductible. We recommend speaking with your accountant to confirm what applies to your situation. fundr can structure your finance to maximise your tax position.
  • Typically you'll need: proof of identity (driver's licence), proof of business (GST number or NZBN), recent bank statements (3–6 months), and details of the ute you want to buy. Nick will guide you through exactly what's needed for your situation.

Car finance questions answered

Car loan interest rates in NZ typically range from 7% to 13% depending on your credit profile, the vehicle's age, loan term, and lender. fundr clients with strong credit histories often secure rates at the lower end of this range. New vehicles and shorter loan terms generally attract lower rates than older used vehicles on longer terms. Use the calculator above to model your specific scenario.

Enter your loan amount, estimated interest rate, and loan term into fundr's calculator above — your monthly repayment updates instantly. The calculator uses the standard PMT formula: P × r(1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n − 1), where P is the principal, r is the monthly rate, and n is the number of months. You can also use the rate table above for quick comparisons across common scenarios.

A 36-month loan means higher monthly payments but significantly less total interest — on a $40,000 loan at 9%, you'd pay roughly $1,271/month and about $5,750 in total interest. A 60-month loan reduces that to $830/month, but total interest grows to around $9,800. Choose 36 months if you can afford it; choose 60 months if cashflow is the priority.

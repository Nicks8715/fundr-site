Privacy Policy

fundr Limited — FSP1007390 — New Zealand

Introduction

fundr Limited (“fundr”, “we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

By using our website at fundr.co.nz or by engaging our services, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.

What Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Full name, date of birth, and contact details (email, phone, address)
  • Business name, trading history, and financial information
  • Driver's licence and identity documents
  • Bank statements, financial accounts, and GST returns
  • Information about the asset you are seeking to finance
  • Any other information you provide when completing an application or contacting us

Information collected automatically

  • IP address and browser type when visiting our website
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referral source

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Assess your application for asset finance and identify suitable lenders
  • Submit your application to lenders on your behalf
  • Communicate with you about your application and our services
  • Comply with our legal and regulatory obligations as an FSP-registered financial services provider
  • Improve our services and website
  • Contact you about relevant products and services (with your consent)

Sharing Your Information

We will share your personal information with lenders on our panel when processing your finance application. We only share information that is necessary for the purpose of assessing and settling your application.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Lenders and credit providers on our panel (with your knowledge and consent)
  • Credit reporting agencies as required
  • Our professional advisers (accountants, lawyers) where required
  • Regulatory bodies as required by law

Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from loss, unauthorised access, use, modification, or disclosure. Your information is stored securely and access is limited to those who need it to perform their duties.

Your Rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Ask us to stop using your information for direct marketing
  • Make a complaint to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at nick@fundr.co.nz.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect the functionality of our website.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

Contact us

Our Story

Why fundr
exists.

fundr was built on a simple observation: most Kiwi businesses were being underserved when it came to asset finance. The banks were slow, the process was opaque, and businesses were left waiting weeks for decisions on equipment they needed now.

Nick Sevta founded fundr to fix that. Having spent 15+ years inside institutions like BNZ, GE Capital, and Wells Fargo, he understood exactly why the system was broken — and how to navigate around it.

As an independent broker, fundr has no allegiance to any lender. We take your application to the right lenders for your deal, structure it to give it the best chance of approval, and get you a decision fast. Most within 24 hours.

The result is a business built on relationships, not transactions. Many of our clients have been with us for years — coming back every time they need a new vehicle, a piece of equipment, or a funding solution for their next step.
